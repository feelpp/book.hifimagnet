Conditions

We consider the conductor as a solenoid with finite thickness and infinite length. This allow us to ignore the z components in our equations. We admit that there is only a radial expansion.

Equations

Taking back the equations in the Maths for Hifimagnet, we consider :

\[ div\sigma+\textbf{j}\times\textbf{b}=0 \]

With the conditions set in the previous section, we have :

\[ -\sigma_{\theta}+\frac{\partial}{\partial r}(r\sigma_{r})=-rj_{\theta}b_{z} \]
